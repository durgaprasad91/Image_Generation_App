# AI Usage in Project Development

This document outlines how Artificial Intelligence (AI) tools were utilized during the development of this project. AI assistance was leveraged for specific cases through platforms like Cursor and occasionally for general programming queries.

## Instances of AI Assistance:

1.  **Debugging and Error Resolution**: AI was notably helpful in diagnosing and resolving complex and persistent errors, particularly during the initial attempts to configure Tailwind CSS with PostCSS and Vite. It assisted in interpreting error messages and suggesting potential fixes.

2.  **Code Refactoring and Organization**: AI provided guidance during the refactoring of existing code, such as moving utility functions and reorganizing component and style files into a more structured directory layout. This included assistance with updating import paths consistently.

3.  **Styling Implementation (Plain CSS)**: While the overall styling approach was my decision to use plain CSS, AI offered suggestions for class names and structural approaches to achieve a clean and modern design within individual component CSS files, especially during the transition away from Tailwind CSS.

4.  **Accessibility Guidance**: AI provided insights and suggestions for implementing accessibility features, including keyboard navigation, visible focus states, and appropriate ARIA attributes for interactive elements.

5.  **Documentation Drafting**: AI was used to draft initial outlines and content for both the `README.md` and this `AI_USAGE.md` file, which I then reviewed and refined.

## Developer's Note:

My primary role as the developer involved making all critical design and implementation decisions, writing the core logic, and ensuring the project requirements were met. AI tools served as an auxiliary resource to accelerate certain tasks and provide problem-solving support, with all outputs thoroughly reviewed and integrated by me.
